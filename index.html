üìã Lead Management Workflow

Step 1: Lead Capture and Entry

The lead capture process is the foundation of your patient pipeline. Every potential patient interaction must be documented in the CRM system to ensure follow-up and conversion tracking.

  1. Log into Zoho CRM Dashboard using your assigned credentials
  2. Navigate to 'Leads' module from the main navigation menu
  3. Click 'Create Lead' button located in the top-right corner
  4. Enter First Name and Last Name in respective fields (required)
  5. Input Mobile Phone number with country code format: +966-XXX-XXX-XXXX
  6. Provide valid Email Address for automated communications
  7. Complete Address fields including street, city, state, and postal code
  8. Select 'Lead Source' from dropdown: Website, Referral, Walk-in, Social Media, Advertisement
  9. Choose 'Service Interest' based on patient inquiry: Dental, Cosmetic, General Consultation
  10. Set 'Lead Status' to 'New' for fresh inquiries
  11. Add 'Next Follow-up Date' - typically within 24-48 hours
  12. Enter detailed Description capturing conversation context and patient needs
  13. Click 'Save' button to create the lead record
‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Fields to Complete:

First Name, Last Name, Mobile Phone, Email, Lead Source, Service Interest, and Description are mandatory.

Step 2: Lead Qualification Process

Qualifying leads efficiently separates serious prospects from casual inquiries.

  1. Open Zoho CRM and navigate to 'Leads' module
  2. Filter leads by 'Lead Status: New'
  3. Review complete lead information before making contact
  4. Click 'Call' button to initiate contact
  5. Introduce yourself: "Hello, this is [Name] from Crystal Shine Clinic"
  6. Reference their inquiry and ask open-ended questions
  7. Document conversation details in 'Notes' section
  8. Assess qualification using NBAT criteria
  9. Update 'Lead Status' to 'Contacted'
  10. Schedule consultation appointment if qualified

üéØ Qualification Criteria (NBAT Framework):

Need: Patient has specific, identifiable service requirement
Budget: Patient can afford treatment or has insurance coverage
Authority: Speaking with decision-maker
Timeline: Patient ready to proceed within 30-90 days

Lead conversion transforms a prospect into an active patient record.

  1. Open the qualified lead record

  3. Click 'Convert' button

  5. Verify patient information
  6. Choose account option
  7. Enter Account Name

  9. Click 'Convert' to finalize

Step 1: Scheduling New Appointments

Appointment scheduling ensures optimal clinic operations and minimal patient wait times.

  1. Log into Zoho CRM with reception credentials
  2. Navigate to 'Events' module
  3. Click 'Create Event' or use Calendar view
  4. Enter Event Title: "[Patient Name] - [Service]"
  5. Select Event Type
  6. Set Start Date and Time
  7. Set appointment duration
  8. Assign to physician
  9. Link to patient contact
  10. Specify clinic location
  11. Enable email reminder
  12. Enable SMS reminder
  13. Add appointment notes
  14. Set staff reminder
  15. Save and confirm appointment

‚è±Ô∏è Appointment Duration Guidelines:

Initial Consultation: 45-60 minutes
Follow-up Visit: 15-30 minutes
Dental Cleaning: 45-60 minutes
Dental Filling: 60-90 minutes
Cosmetic Consultation: 30-45 minutes
Minor Procedure: 30-60 minutes
Major Procedure: 90-180 minutes
Emergency Visit: 15-30 minutes
‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Scheduling Rules:

  • Immediately notify physician for emergency slots

  4. Verify physician availability
  5. Check room availability
  6. Add reschedule note
  7. Update reminders
  8. Save changes
  9. System sends updated confirmation
